Our Audience

Who is our audience?

My audience is primarily individuals who enjoy gourmet sweets and confections, particularly those who appreciate high-quality, handmade products. This includes food enthusiasts, gift shoppers, and people looking for unique treats for special occasions.

Where is our audience located?

My audience is located throughout the nation, with a focus on urban and suburban areas where there is a higher concentration of food enthusiasts and shoppers looking for specialty items.

What is their age range?

The age range of my audience is quite broad, typically ranging from young adults in their 20s to older adults in their 60s. However, the core demographic is likely between 25 and 45 years old, as this group tends to have more disposable income to spend on gourmet treats.

Is it targeted more towards women or men?

The audience is targeted slightly more towards women, as they are often the primary shoppers for household and specialty food items. However, the products are designed to appeal to all!
