Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

The marketplace for the Haddy, a reusable sweat liner for your hat!

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Images will be taken by my client and text will be written by my client as well.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care about the focus of the site because it is a small business that is trying to grow and reach more customers. The website will allow users to view and purchase the Haddy, as well as learn more about the product, how it's made, the business and its values.

Why should a site be built for this?

A site should be built for this because it will allow the business to reach a wider audience and provide a convenient way for customers to purchase the Haddy. It will also help to establish the brand and create a professional image for the business.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to browse the selection of Haddy products, learn more about the business, and make a purchase.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to increase sales and grow the customer base for The Haddy.
