Our Competition

Who is our competition?

Our competition is other small businesses that sell reusable sweat liners for hats, such as local hat shops and online outdoor gear stores.

What do they do well?

They have established brand recognition and strong distribution due to how long they've been in business, but their product does well also due to it's disposability.

What do we do better?

While our competitors have great outreach and a good reputation, their product isn't reusable and is not environmentally friendly like ours is. By making a sweatliner that's washable, you can keep using it over and over again, saving money and reducing waste.
