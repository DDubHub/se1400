Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

A shop of Marshelle's Caramels; delicious, homemade sweets that can be viewed and purchased by everyone throughout the nation!

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Images will be taken by myself and text will be written by myself.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care about the focus of the site because it is a small business that is trying to grow and reach more customers. The website will allow users to view and purchase the caramels, as well as learn more about the business and its values.

Why should a site be built for this?

A site should be built for this because it will allow the business to reach a wider audience and provide a convenient way for customers to purchase the caramels. It will also help to establish the brand and create a professional image for the business.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to browse the selection of caramels, learn more about the business, and make a purchase.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to increase sales and grow the customer base for Marshelle's Caramels.
