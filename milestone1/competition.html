Our Competition and Why They're Not Really Competition

Who is our competition?

My competition is other small businesses that sell homemade caramels and sweets, such as local candy shops and online confectionery stores, along with larger commpanies like Kraft and Werther's.

What do they do well?

They have established brand recognition and strong distribution due to how long they've been in business.

What do we do better?

While our competitors have great outreach, the product isn't nearly as pure as ours. Nothing can beat a tried and true family recipe thats been around longer than any of our competitors.
